About Me

Puzzle Solver

Hello! I'm Kenneth, a web developer with experience in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, and React.js.
Growing up, I loved logical problem-solving. I can still remember the excitement I felt as a child beating video games, like The Legend of Zelda, that involved puzzle-solving, and solving mysteries while reading novels, like Sherlock Holmes, of that genre. In college, I majored in mathematics, and afterwards, I pursued an master's degree in math education to spread that love of puzzle-solving to others. I gained insight on how people at various levels of understanding approach problem-solving while learning new problem-solving techniques. Even now, outside of web development, I puzzle-solve for fun. I go indoor bouldering twice a week when time allows and practice problem-solving on the wall.
